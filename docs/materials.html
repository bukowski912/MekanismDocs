Materials

Mekanism espouses a variety of materials, each of which can exist in one or more forms. They are listed below.

Chemicals

Chemicals are transportable by Pressurized Tubing. They may be stored in Chemical Tanks; however, those that are radioactive may only be stored in Radioactive Waste Barrels. Radioactive chemicals are highlighted below.

Application-list (by relevance)

Chemical Made By Used By
Hydrogen
  • Electrolytic Separator using Water
  • Gas-Burning Generator to make power
  • Jetpack for fuel
  • Armored Jetpack for fuel
  • Chemical Infuser to make Hydrogen Chloride
  • Pressurized Reaction Chamber to make Substrate and Ethylene
Oxygen
  • Electrolytic Separator using Water
  • Optionally replaceable by flint on some machines
  • Purification Chamber to make Clumps
  • Chemical Infuser to make Sulfur Trioxide
  • Scuba Tank as breathable supply
Steam
  • Fission reactor as heated coolant (from Water)
  • Industrial Turbine to make power
  • Chemical Injection Chamber for crafting
Water Vapor
  • Rotary Condensentrator using Water
  • Chemical Infuser to make Sulfuric Acid
  • Chemical Injection Chamber with Dirt to make Clay
Ethene / Ethylene
  • Pressurized Reaction Chamber using Bio Fuel
  • Gas-Burning Generator to make power
Brine
  • Thermal Evaporation Plant using Water
  • Electrolytic Separator to make Sodium and Chlorine
  • Thermal Evaporation Plant to make Lithium
Sodium
  • Electrolytic Separator using Brine
  • Sodium cooled Fission Reactor setup
Superheated Sodium
  • Fission Reactor using Sodium as a coolant
  • Thermoelectric Boiler to condense into Sodium
Chlorine
  • Electrolytic Separator using Brine
  • Chemical Infuser to make Hydrogen Chloride
Lithium
  • Thermal Evaporation Plant using Brine
  • Solar Neutron Activator to make Tritium
  • Chemical Crystallizer to make Lithium Dust
Sulfur Dioxide
  • Chemical Oxidizer using Sulfur
  • Chemical Infuser to make Sulfur Trioxide
Sulfur Trioxide
  • Chemical Infuser using Oxygen and Sulfur Dioxide
  • Chemical Infuser to make Sulfuric Acid
Sulfuric Acid
  • Chemical Infuser using Sulfur Trioxide and Water Vapor
  • Chemical Dissolution Chamber to make Slurry
Hydrogen Chloride
  • Chemical Infuser using Hydrogen and Chlorine
  • Chemical Injection Chamber to make Shards
  • Chemical Injection Chamber to make Sulfur
Hydrofluoric Acid
  • Chemical Dissolution Chamber using Sulfuric Acid and Fluorite
  • Chemical Infuser with Uranium Oxide to make Uranium Hexafluoride
Uranium Oxide
  • Chemical Oxidizer using Yellow Cake Uranium
  • Chemical Infuser with Hydrofluoric Acid to make Uranium Hexafluoride
Uranium Hexafluoride
  • Chemical Infuser using Hydrofluoric Acid
  • Isotopic Centrifuge to make Fissile Fuel
Fissile Fuel
  • Isotopic Centrifuge using Hydrofluoric Acid
  • Fission Reactor to make power and Nuclear Waste
Nuclear Waste
  • Fission Reactor using Fissile Fuel
  • Solar Neutron Activator to make Polonium and Spent Nuclear Waste
  • Isotopic Centrifuge to make Plutonium and Spent Nuclear Waste
Spent Nuclear Waste
  • Pressurized Reaction Chamber with Polonium, Water and Fluorite Dust
 Nothing.

Polonium

  • Pressurized Reaction Chamber with Water to make Polonium Pellet
Antimatter
